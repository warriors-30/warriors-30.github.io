About Me

I am a third-year master's student at Cooperative Medianet Innovation Center (CMIC), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Jiangchao Yao. I was a research intern at RIKEN AIP, fortunately working with Prof. Gang Niu and Prof. Masashi Sugiyama, and I was a research assistant at TMLR group of Hong Kong Baptist University, fortunately working with Prof. Bo Han. Before that, I received my B.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2023.

My research interests lie in trustworthy, efficient and agentic AI, with the recent focus on topics as follows:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • (Multi-modal) Diffusion LLMs
  • Efficient Training and Inference
  • LLM-based Agents
